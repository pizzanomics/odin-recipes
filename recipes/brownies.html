Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Baking powder
  • Honey
  • Confectioner's sugar

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove, then remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  2. Beat in the dry ingredients. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
  3. Stir the frosting ingredients together until well-combined, then frost the brownies while they are still warm.
